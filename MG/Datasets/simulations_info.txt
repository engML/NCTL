- Finite Volume Method
- Second-order descritization
- 480 train data (+28 for second dataset) using simulations at 25x25, 50x50, 200x200, and 400x400 uniform and structured grid system.
- 100 test data using simulations at 400x400 uniform and structured grid system.

In the csv files, the first and second columns are the Rayleigh and Prandtl numbers, respectively.
In the csv files, the third number in a row is the data for the average Nusselt number on the vertical walls.
